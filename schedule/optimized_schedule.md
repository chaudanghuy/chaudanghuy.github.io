## Time Management Tips

### Daily Routine
1. **Morning**: Review today's goals during commute
2. **Lunch**: Watch 1 tutorial video (15 min)
3. **After Work**: 30-minute break before task job
4. **Learning**: Focus blocks with 25-minute Pomodoro
5. **Before Sleep**: Review progress and plan tomorrow

### Weekend Optimization
- **Saturday**: Deep work on 2nd job (most energy)
- **Sunday**: Creative work on game startup (more relaxed)

### Productivity Hacks
- Use time blocking (schedule fixed slots)
- No social media during learning hours
- Keep phone in another room
- Use noise-canceling headphones
- Track time with Toggl or RescueTime

### Burnout Prevention
- Take 1 complete rest day/month
- Exercise 3-4 times/week (even 20 min helps)
- Sleep 7-8 hours (non-negotiable)
- If feeling overwhelmed, reduce game startup time temporarily
